DISABILITY

Disability results from the interaction between individuals with a health condition, such as Cerebral Palsy, Down Syndrome and Depression, with personal and environmental factors including negative attitudes, inaccessible transportation and public buildings, and limited social support. Know More

OUR STORY

Childhood disorders have become common. The prevalence of childhood disorders is more in Bihar as compared to other states in the country. People are struggling to find best medical care for their kids.

In the view of above situation and the growing problems and their impact, We came up with "Helping-Hand"as an initiative to provide a complete medical treatment, with better accessibility.

We serve children with special needs.

OUR APPROACH

We work for the overall development in a child. This can be ensured by making the child indulge in normal activities of playing, learning, talking, speaking and others.
